About Me

Hello! I'm Bar Amsalem, 29 years old, product manager.

As a technology professional with a passion for people, business and finance, I bring a unique perspective to the world of product management.

My educational background includes a B.Sc. in computer science from the College of Management, plus a CCNA certification and a product management certificate from the Technion. With 4 years of experience in project management, support and operations, I had the opportunity to work with leading hi-tech companies as well as entrepreneurship and production in the world of culture and events. This diverse background sharpened my process management skills and deepened my understanding of user needs and business dynamics. In this blog, I aim to tell about myself, share insights from the world of technology and finance and offer valuable perspectives on the challenges and opportunities in the emerging industry. I invite you to discuss innovative ideas and exchange knowledge about the digital landscape. Feel free to share your experiences or leave details for collaborations. Together, we can navigate the complexities of technology and innovation, fostering a community of continuous learning and growth.
